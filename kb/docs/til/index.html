    • [vim] Convert line indention from 2 to 4 spaces

      Convert two-space indents to tabs:

      :set ts=2 sts=2 noet
:retab!

      Convert tabs to four-space indents:

      :set ts=4 sts=4 et
:retab

    • [python3] Merging two dictionaries in Python 3.x.

      $ python3
>>> xs = {"a": 1, "b": 2}
>>> ys = {"a": 9, "c": 3}
>>> z = {**xs, **ys}
>>> z
{'a': 9, 'b': 2, 'c': 3}
